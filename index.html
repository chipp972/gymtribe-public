Get the App See Features
Features

Everything you need in the gym

Built for athletes who want full control over their training and nutrition, without the bloat.

🏋️

Workout Logging

Log every set, rep, and weight with a fast, gesture-driven interface built for use mid-workout.

📋

Program Templates

Follow science-backed programs — 5/3/1, PPL, Upper/Lower, 5×5 — or build your own from scratch.

🥗

Nutrition Tracking

Log meals, scan barcodes, and track macros daily. See projections against your nutrition goals.

⏱️

Rest Timers

Configurable rest intervals per exercise with audio cues and background playback support.

📈

Progress Analysis

Charts per exercise, muscle group heatmaps, and session history to see your gains over time.

💾

Offline & Private

All data stored locally on your device. No account required. Full export/import for backups.
